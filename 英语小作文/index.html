如何表达歉意

I must apologize to you for lossing your book you lend me the last week

我必须向你道歉，因为我把你上周借给我的书弄丢了

I feel so sorry that i can’t take part in your birthday party this Saturday

我对我周六不能参加你的生日聚会感到抱歉

Please accept my apology for having delivered to you a bottle of expired milk

请接受我的道歉因为寄给你了一瓶过期的牛奶

如何让表明写信目的

I am writing here to express my concern about the declining physical condition among our college students

我写这封信是想表达我对我们大学生日益下降的身体状况的担忧
I have made my decision to quit as an edition for Designs & Fashions

我已经决定退出《设计与时尚》的编辑工作
On behalf of the Students’ Union,I am honored that you have chosed pay a visit to our university

我代表学生会，很荣幸您选择来我们学校访问

如何表达感谢

I really appreciate that you have given me a hand during my preparation for IELTS that my score meets the requirement necessary for the application for Cambridge University

我非常感谢您在我备考雅思期间给予我的帮助，我的雅思成绩达到了申请剑桥大学最低分数要求

I want to express my thanks to you for helping with my business these days

我想对你这些天帮助我打理生意表达谢意

Let me expend my sincere gratitude for sending my lost daughter home

请允许我对你把我走失的女儿送回家表达感激之情

I must tell you that your colleagues and you were so hospitalized that I almost felt at home during my stay in your honored school

不得不说，您和您的同事对我非常热情，在贵学院逗留期间感觉像在家里一样

我非常关心你父母的健康
